📋 Finding Your Parameter Values

Use this guide to locate the required values from your medical reports:

Parameter Report/Source Typical Units How to Find
Age Patient Demographics Years Current age in years
Serum Bilirubin Liver Function Test (LFT) mg/dL Look for "Total Bilirubin" or "Serum Bilirubin" in LFT report
Albumin Liver Function Test (LFT) g/dL Look for "Serum Albumin" in LFT report
Platelet Count Complete Blood Count (CBC) per µL (or 10³/μL) Look for "Platelet Count" or "PLT" in CBC report
Prothrombin Time Coagulation Profile Seconds Look for "PT" or "Prothrombin Time" in coagulation studies

🔍 Where to Find These Values

Liver Function Test (LFT):

Contains Bilirubin and Albumin values. Usually labeled as "LFT" or "Liver Panel"

Complete Blood Count (CBC):

Contains Platelet Count. Usually labeled as "CBC" or "Hemogram"

Coagulation Profile:

Contains Prothrombin Time. May be labeled as "PT/INR" or "Coagulation Studies"

Physical Exam:

Age is simply your current age in years
